About

Thank you for using the MSI Achievement Management System.

This application was built to counter human limitaions in terms of achievement manaagement. This platform provides a place to showcase your achievements and also help the college manaagement in ease of information access. You can link your achievements in your resume or portfolio by copying the permanent link to your achievement. This website is completely open source; feel free to contribute to our GitHub repositories. You can find the source code at GitHub:
  • Frontend
  • Backend API

NOTE: Achievements you add will be visible on the home page once they are approved by the MSI faculty.
